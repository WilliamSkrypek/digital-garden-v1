# Efforts
Below is a list of things I am currently working on or involved in.

2021 - Present  
**Minnesota Youth Symphonies (MYS)**  
A school-year orchestra that meets on Saturday mornings at Macalester College to rehearse professional-level orchestral music for three seasonal concerts. I am currently a member of the Repertory Orchestra under the direction of Lucas Shogren

*I also hold the position of Student Representative for the Repertory Orchestra to the MYS student leadership committee*

---
2025 - Present  
**Greater Twin Cities Youth Symphonies (gtcys)**  
Another school-year orchestra that meets weekly on Tuesday nights at Westminster Presbyterian Church, to rehearse symphonies and other orchestral music, for many seasonal and charity concerts. I am currently a member of the Philharmonic Orchestra under the direction of Mark Russell Smith.

Washburn Highschool's track and field team. Last season I ran for the Varsity Hurdles team, competing in the 110 meter hurdles and the 300 meter hurdles races.
